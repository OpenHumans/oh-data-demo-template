How it works

  1. Click the button below
    This takes you to Open Humans authentification page.
  2. Log in or create an Open Humans account
    Your 'source' data will be added to this account.
  3. Authorize this app in Open Humans
    This authorizes us to add your 'source' data to your account.
  4. Authorize us to access your 'source' account.
    The Open Humans website will redirect you back to us for the last step: Authorizing us to access your 'source' account.

FAQ

  1. Will my data from 'source' be public?
    The short answer: No, by connecting your account you don't agree to make your data public or give anyone access to this data. It will be stored in your Open Humans account and you decide with whom to share.
    The longer answer: It can be public if you want it to be public. If you're logged into Open Humans can make individual sources publicly available through the public data feature.
  2. Do you keep my data up to date?
    Yes, we regularly update your data to keep it up to date. After logging in you can also manually trigger an update.
